Meet the Hydration Cowboy, a former Louisiana frog named Ethan "Ez" Springs, transformed by a water spirit into a man with a mission to hydrate the parched corners of the world. Leaping across the plains with his canteen-holsters, he's an unlikely hero, educating everyone he meets on the importance of hydration. Over time, this amphibious cowboy's tireless efforts have turned him into a legend, symbolizing his belief: "Hydration is a right, not a fight!"

This ain't your everyday cowboy..

Ethan harbors an adoration for his loyal hen, Clara, that once saw him overhydrate her in a fit of overzealous care, a slip-up that provoked Clara's feathery fury and left him on the receiving end of an irate clucking lecture.
Once, at a local birthday party, Ethan rode around on a faux pony, preaching his "hydrate or diedrate" mantra to a horde of sugar-fueled kids, an endeavor that culminated in a chaotic water-balloon uprising.
On his transformation day, Ethan discovered he wasn't alone; his cousin had also experienced the same metamorphosis, and they commemorated their shared destiny with a jubilant photo, forever capturing their bond as the hydration cowboys.
Hydro Frog, eager to boost bottle sales, approached Ethan for a collaboration, leading to an iconic photo shoot featuring the amphibious cowboy straddling a gargantuan hydration bottle.

"Whether in swampland or desert, under the burning sun or the pale moonlight, remember, partner, hydration is a right, not a fight!"

- Ethan "Ez" Springs aka Hydration Cowboy
