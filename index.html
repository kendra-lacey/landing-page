Meet the Hydration Cowboy, a former Louisiana frog named Ethan "Ez" Springs, transformed by a water spirit into a man with a mission to hydrate the parched corners of the world. Leaping across the plains with his canteen-holsters, he's an unlikely hero, educating everyone he meets on the importance of hydration. Over time, this amphibious cowboy's tireless efforts have turned him into a legend, symbolizing his belief: "Hydration is a right, not a fight!"
